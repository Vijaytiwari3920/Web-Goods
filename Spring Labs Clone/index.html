Security & Privacy for Industries and Governance

SPRInG Labs, led by Dr. Souradyuti Paul is dedicated to pioneering research in blockchain security, cryptography, and machine learning to drive innovation and ensure security solutions for modern industries and governance systems. The focus of the lab activities is as much on theoretical analyses of security solutions as on end-to-end product development with modern security features.
